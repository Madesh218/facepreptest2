Here are some of our top-3 trendig movies...

A Different Man

Back in January, I happened upon a last-minute ticket to the Sundance premiere of A Different Man. I knew next to nothing heading to the Eccles Theater besides a murky sentence or two that I’d read in the festival’s program — something about an aspiring actor who undergoes an experimental medical procedure. Close to a third of the way through, I still had no clue what exactly I was watching. By the time “I Can’t Hardly Stand It” by The Cramps started playing in a rowdy post-op bar scene, I was officially all in and couldn’t wait for things to get even weirder. Don’t dilute the medicinal value, go into this movie knowing as little as possible! — Sean B.

Man With Movie Camera

"He says you'll never be hurt as much by being open as you have been by remaining closed." The messenger is a school janitor with a master's in art history who claims to be channeling "from both sides of the veil." "He" is Adam, a three-year-old who has never spoken an intelligible word. And the message is intended for Martha Beck, Adam's mother, who doesn't know whether to make a mad dash for the door to escape a raving lunatic (after all, how many conversations like this one can you have before you stop getting dinner party invitations and start pushing a mop yourself?) or accept another in a series of life lessons from an impeccable but mysterious source.
